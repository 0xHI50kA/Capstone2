About

Welcome to the AI Symptom Checker! This tool helps you assess your symptoms or those of your loved ones. Using advanced AI, we analyze your input to provide potential health guidance.

How to Use

  • Step 1: Fill out the details prompted by the chatbot, such as age, sex, and symptoms.
  • Step 2: Answer a few Yes/No questions about the symptoms.
  • Step 3: Receive a potential diagnosis or health guidance.
