Health Topics

What Is Cough

    What is a cough? Coughing is a protective reflex. It can remove dust and other things that have been breathed in. A cough can also get rid of mucus from your throat and chest. Coughing is how your body cleans the lungs and airways.

What symptoms are related to a cough?

    The symptoms that come with a cough will vary. It depends on what is causing your cough. Coughing that lasts for more than 3 weeks may also be a sign of a health problem. See your doctor if there is no other reason for your cough.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Question

What are your office hours? +

We are available in the office Monday thru Friday from 8:00am-5:00pm. A highly trained manager is available during non-business hours to receive your call personally.

What services does Atab Health Center offer? +

Atab Health Center offers a wide range of healthcare services, including Infant Immunization, Family Planning, and TB DOTS. We also provide Dental Check-ups and General Consultation for adults, children, and infants. Our maternal care services include Pre-Natal, Post-Partum, and Newborn Check-ups. Additionally, we offer Senior Consultation, Breast and Cervical Screening, and Adolescent Consultation. For patients who require medical attention at home, we also conduct Home Visitation services.

Do you provide home Visit? +

Yes. For over two decades, Personal Health Care has been serving the Pediatric Special Needs population. Our staff has extensive experience and provides excellent care and understanding for these individuals.
